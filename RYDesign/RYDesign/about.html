Origin of RY Design

Ricky’s story began in 1990 when he started his research project on new speaker technology that can deliver the sound quality that he wanted. However, after 4 years of research and development, he wasn’t able to achieve sound quality that he wanted and abandoned his research in favour of opening an electronics store.

Even after he formally abandoned his project, Ricky continued his research and development project whilst running his electronics store. In 1999, he finally created a sound system consisting of a 16 cm tall satellite speaker and numerous miniature satellite speaker. He quickly patented his design before anyone could copy his design.

Determined to market his design, Ricky closed down his electronics store and opened a new dedicated store to sell his design. He emptied his savings, sold his car, and got loans from multiple sources to fund his new store. The production of his speaker was all done by hand with the help of two employees. A year after the new store was opened, Ricky’s investment began to pay off as customers began buying his speakers. His clientele include the former Indonesian Minister of Trade and a successful business called Rahmat Gobel, who also helped Ricky to expand his business.

Until now, Ricky continue to produce his speaker by hand in his private home in Tangerang with the help of eleven employees. Apart from his original design, Ricky also offers a turbo bass speaker that can be used in large halls. He also offers the world’s only 400 Watt professional bass speaker that has the same performance as 800-1,200 Watt speakers.

Ricky is now planning to produce his speakers at an industrial scale at the Panasonic plant. He already secured loans from the bank to make his dream come true.

Kisah Ricky dimulai di tahun 1990, saat dia memulai riset untuk membuat sebuah speaker yang bisa membuat kualitas suara yang dia inginkan. Tetapi setelah 4 tahun, Ricky tidak berhasil untuk menemukan sebuah speaker yang dia inginkan dan akhirnya menyetop risetnya.

Meskipun dia sudah berhenti bekerja dalam risetnya, Ricky terus mengembangkan proyek dia sambil menjalankan toko elektronik dia di Glodok. Di tahun 1999, dia akhirnya berhasil membuat sebuah sound sistem yang terdiri oleh sebuah speaker satelit yang tingginya 16 cm dan beberapa speaker satelit miniatur. Dia segera mematenkan desainya sebelum orang lain bis membajak desain dia.

Ingin untuk memperkenalkan desain dia, Ricky menutup toko elektronik dia dan membuka sebuah toko khusus untuk speaker. Untuk mendirikan toko baru tersebut, dia menguras tabungan dia, menjual mobil dia, dan meminjam uang dari sana-sini. Produksi speaker barunya dikerjakan dengan tangan dan dengan bantuan dua pegawai. Satu tahun setelah pembukaan toko baru dia, investasi Ricky akhirnya mulai berbuah. Orang-orang mulai membeli speaker buatan dia dan dia bisa menjual 20 speaker per bulan. Salah satu pelanggan dia adalah Rahmat Gobel, mantan menteri perdagangan dan seorang pengusaha. Rahmat Gobel juga membantu Ricky untuk mengembangkan bisnisnya.

Sampai sekarang, Ricky masih membuat speaker dia dengan tangan, dibantu oleh sebelas pegawai. Selain desain orisinal dia, Ricky juga menjual speaker turbo bass yang bisa digunakan dalam ruang berskala besar. Dia juga membuat satu-satunya speaker bass professional didunia yang hanya mengkonsumsi 400 Watt tapi memiliki kemampuan yang setara dengan speaker yang memakan 800-1,200 Watt.

Sekarang, Ricky sedang berencana untuk memproduksi speaker dia dalam skala besar di pabrik Panasonic. Untuk membuat impian dia menjadi kenyataan, Ricky sudah mendapatkan pinjaman dari bank.
